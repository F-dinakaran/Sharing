Heartogether Terms and Conditions

Heartogether is a communication platform designed for educational and non-emergency communication between cardiac patients and healthcare professionals.

1. Not an emergency service

Heartogether must not be used for medical emergencies. In case of chest pain, breathing difficulty, fainting, severe symptoms, or urgent medical danger, users must contact emergency services immediately.

2. Medical information

Information shared through the platform should be accurate and respectful. Doctors and patients are responsible for the information they provide.

3. Privacy and communication

Private chats are intended to be visible only between the selected doctor and patient. Community chats are visible to all registered users.

4. Appropriate use

Users must not share offensive, harmful, misleading, abusive or inappropriate content.

5. Educational project notice

This version of Heartogether is a student project/demo and should not be used as a real clinical healthcare system.
